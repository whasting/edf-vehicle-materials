About This Project

System Overview

Roof-mounted electric ducted fans provide forward thrust to reduce aerodynamic drag and improve highway fuel economy on a 2010 Toyota RAV4.

Configuration

  • EDFs: 2× JP Hobby 120mm (12-blade metal, 760KV)
  • Thrust per EDF: 16.5 lbs @ 4,500W (100% throttle)
  • Operating thrust: ~11 lbs @ 2,400W (53% throttle)
